Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Important: This Privacy Policy explains how Promin3000 ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website and use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address when you use our contact form
  • Communication Data: Messages you send through our contact form
  • Account Information: If you create an account for music licensing or collaborations

1.2 Technical Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral sources
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device for site functionality

1.3 TikTok Integration Data

When interacting with TikTok-related features:

  • Public profile information (username, display name)
  • Public content metadata (video titles, descriptions)
  • Engagement metrics (views, likes - public data only)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: To respond to inquiries and provide music services
  • Communication: To send updates about collaborations and new releases
  • Website Improvement: To analyze usage and enhance user experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Marketing: To promote new music and services (with your consent)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and email services
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Partners: For music distribution and licensing (with consent)
  • TikTok: Only as permitted by TikTok's API Terms of Service

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure hosting with reputable providers
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand website usage
  • Marketing Cookies: For targeted advertising (with consent)

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Age Restrictions

Age Requirement: Our services are intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 16 and in the EU, you need parental consent.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

10. TikTok Compliance

Our TikTok integration complies with:

  • TikTok API Terms of Service
  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • We only access public data as permitted by TikTok's API

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications for material changes

12. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@promin3000.com
  • Website: Through our contact form
  • Response Time: We will respond within 30 days
